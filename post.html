Water. The pathway to Life and Death.

The road to life may end up at death's door.

Posted by Dragan on August 12, 1992

As I walk down to the brewery to get some water, I pass by many others, both dead and alive, on the same journey. To retrieve the most essential element of life, water. Many have perished on this trip, killed by the men on the hills with the guns and bombs. All because they wanted to fill their mouths with life.

I am no different. I walk down this familar path to where I know I will get water, knowing in doing so, I am risking my life. If the men on the hills decide this is my final trip, then I have a very big choice in whether it is or isn't.

Our survival depends on whether or not we get water, and whether or not we get our water depends on the men on the hills, and how they are feeling. Maybe one day they are feeling generous, and let most of us live as we pass their gaze. Or maybe one day they are feeling tyranical, and kill as much of us as they can.

The journey to our salvation may lead us to unexpected delays.
