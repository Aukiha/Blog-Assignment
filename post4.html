Let's Keep Going.

Let us not give in to the men on the hills' actions, and keep moving forward.

Posted by Dragan on November 22, 1992

This war has been going on for months now. Many have already died. I have not seen my family, wife and children, since shortly before all this started. I struggle every day to survive. And yet I will not give up. For giving up means to have gave up to the men on the hills, which I will not do.

This war has changed our city and ourselves. But in this time of crisis, we should not be considering stopping, and letting our very existence end like our old Sarajevo did. We must continue struggling, through each and every day, until we come out of this war alive, or die of our own volition.

If the men on the hills will fight us with their guns, then we will fight them with our actions, and our thoughts, and our survival. We will show them that we will not let their eradication of Sarajevo continue, and that we will survive this war, and that justice will be served for all those who have been lost already.

Let us continue surviving, and after all this conflict, rebuild our Sarajevo.
